`Multimodal AI` `NLP` `Semantic Search` `AI Agents`

### 🌊 AI-Driven Hybrid Ocean Energy System

Exploring the use of artificial intelligence to intelligently manage a hybrid renewable energy system combining **wave energy and ocean thermal energy**.
